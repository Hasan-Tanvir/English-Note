Import on Phone

Later on your phone:

  1. Open this app on your phone
  2. Go to Sync tab
  3. Click "Import from File"
  4. Select the file you exported
  5. All notes will appear on your phone!

Install on Phone

To install as an app on your phone:

Android: Chrome → Menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen"

iPhone: Safari → Share (⎋) → "Add to Home Screen"
